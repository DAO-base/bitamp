Privacy and Transparency on the Blockchain

Many blockchains are inherently transparent places, and because of that, the transactions you complete with Bitamp may be publicly accessible. Any information that a given blockchain stores may not only be public, but immutable as well, and it can be impossible to eliminate completely. Any transaction you complete and any address you use to complete transaction might reveal information about your identity that can be correlated with other information at any point in time by any individual or agency. Be sure you understand how both privacy and transparency work on the blockchain.
